What the colors say: Persona 1 (Personal-AI consumer) is the highest-priority customer and the persona whose surface coverage looks thinnest right now. Most of the dashboard's team-tabs are P2/P3 shaped, the CLI is P3/P4 entirely, and the docs lean P3/P4. P1's natural path through MCP tools (mail+chat + contacts) is well-covered as tools, but the surrounding mental model (teams, roles, identities) is wrong-shaped for her.

What the badges say: Yellow dup badges flag the candidates for the simplification pass — paired auth pages, the two contacts_list / list_contacts verbs, the wrap-tool pair that duplicates mail+chat, the /connect route that exists in two places, and the three first-time-user surfaces.

Methodology note: "P1+P2+P3" coloring on Mail/Chat/Contacts dashboard tabs reflects that all three personas use those, even though the surrounding vocabulary today is team-shaped. The coloring is "does this persona need this capability," not "does this surface speak their language today."
